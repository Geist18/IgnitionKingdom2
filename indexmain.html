    Engine Tune-up

    Our engine tune-ups include checking, diagnosing, and replacing bad spark plugs, spark plug wires, distributor caps, fuel filters, air filters, and oil filters. Included in our sevice is checking of emission levels, fuel lines, wiring, coolant hoses, and serpentine belts.

    Suspension Systems

    We check your tire inflation and tread depth every month, or every 1,000 miles, we check the power steering fluid at every oil change and check all suspension components for wear every 15,000 miles

    Brake Service

    During the maintenance service, your brake fluid will be inspected for proper level and moisture content. Our technician will first perform a brake inspection to make sure all components are working properly. Our service tech will check your brake pads, rotors, clips and calipers to make sure that every component is working as it should.

    Wheel Alignment & Tire Rotation

    Tire rotation is a maintenance procedure that we do which involves moving the tires around the wheel. This can help distribute wear evenly across all four tires and improve gas mileage by balancing the weight distribution of your vehicle. While wheel alignment involves checking and adjusting the suspension.

    Oil Change

    Our oil change service,includes changing of the oil; plus a safety inspection that examines the oil filter, cabin filter, a check of brake pads, tires, fluids, etc. The price for an oil change service is usually higher but you can expect our extra care, value, and reports placed into the inspection. If you are in a pinch and you need an oil change in a hurry, you can get away with a quick oil change without issue.

    Air Conditioning & Heating

    Regular maintenance includes checking hoses and belts, and ensuring the antifreeze is clean and filled to proper levels; it will help keep the whole system in good working order. To ensure your car is able to keep you and your engine cool all summer long.
