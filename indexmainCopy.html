    Our Story

    Ignition Kingdom was founded by two close friends, Karl Brown and Dominic Asico, who shared a passion for cars and a desire to make a positive impact in their community. Dominic had worked as a mechanic for several years, while Karl had experience as a driving instructor, and they both noticed a need for high-quality, affordable maintenance and driving education services in their area. They spent several months developing a business plan and securing funding, and eventually opened Ignition Kingdom in a small industrial building on the outskirts of town. The name "Ignition Kingdom" was chosen to reflect their shared love for all things automotive, as well as their belief that everyone should have the opportunity to learn how to maintain and drive a vehicle safely.

    At first, business was slow, and Karl and Dominic had to rely on word-of-mouth referrals and a few local advertisements to attract customers. However, as their reputation for professionalism and expertise grew, so did their customer base. They expanded their offerings to include courses on basic and advanced vehicle maintenance, defensive driving techniques, and even specialized classes for commercial drivers.

    Over time, Ignition Kingdom became a trusted resource for drivers of all skill levels, and Karl and Dominic were able to hire additional instructors and staff to keep up with demand. They continued to invest in the latest technology and training techniques, and even expanded their physical location to include a full-service garage and repair shop. Today, Ignition Kingdom is a thriving business with a loyal customer base and a reputation for excellence in both maintenance and driving education. Karl and Dominic are proud of what they have built, and are excited to continue serving their community for many years to come.
